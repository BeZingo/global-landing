Take your Kuwaiti Business to the Globe.

The Kuwait Business Council in Dubai and the Northern Emirates in collaboration with “Al Lujain Exhibitions & Events Management Company”, aims to increase the presence of Kuwaiti companies and merchants at the upcoming season of the Global Village, which will run from the 1st of November 2017 to the 7th of April 2018. This will be achieved by receiving applications for participation across various sectors, with the goal of providing an opportunity to introduce Kuwaiti products and culture.

About Global Village.

The Global Village in Dubai, is considered one of the most visited entertaining, cultural & touristic destination of its kind in the region for the varied leisure events and unique shopping experiences it provides visitors, through 30 participating pavilions, representing 75 countries. During its last season the Global Village welcomed 5.6 million visitors and generated transactions worth a total of 2.3 billion dirhams. Additionally, cash and other promotional prizes worth 2 million dirhams were awarded at the Global Village with more than 600,000 toys being won at the fun fair area “Fantasy Island”. 27 concerts by renowned international musicians were hosted at the Global Village, in addition to more than 12,000 cultural and entertaining shows and 78 fireworks displays. The Global Village is located along the Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed road off exit 37.

  • "The Kuwaiti Pavillion"

    The Kuwaiti Pavilion at the “Global Village” represents a journey in Kuwait’s heritage, where visitors will be able to experience a unique atmosphere merging the present with the past. The design of the pavilion reflects the long-standing relationship of the Kuwaiti people with the sea and Kuwait’s fishing heritage, as the pavilion’s entrance is designed in the form of a giant seashell lined with historic traditional houses. Through its exhibits of abayas, perfumes, “bakhour”, “oud” and perfume oils, the pavilion will showcase Kuwait’s rich traditions, in addition to the accompanying cultural and heritage performances that will take place at the pavilion, throughout the 159-day duration of the Global Village.
